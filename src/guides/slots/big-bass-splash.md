## Introduction to Big Bass Splash

Big Bass Splash is the standout entry in Pragmatic Play's hugely popular Big Bass series, developed by Reel Kingdom. Building on the original Big Bass Bonanza formula, Splash adds five selectable free spins modifiers, a four-level progression system, and random bonus features that can transform any spin into a big win.

The fishing theme is executed with charm. Your reels sit above an underwater world filled with aquatic life, and the familiar fisherman returns as the Wild symbol with an important job: collecting every Money Fish value on screen. It is this collect mechanic, combined with escalating multipliers across four levels, that gives Big Bass Splash its explosive win potential of up to 5,000x your stake.

## How Big Bass Splash Works

### Base Game

Big Bass Splash uses a classic 5-reel, 3-row layout with 10 fixed paylines. Wins are formed by landing matching symbols on a payline from left to right, starting from the leftmost reel.

The base game is straightforward, but two key symbols add depth:

- **Money Fish** symbols appear with random cash values attached
- **Fisherman Wild** symbols substitute for all regular symbols and collect the values of any Money Fish visible on screen

When a Fisherman Wild lands on the same spin as one or more Money Fish, the fisherman "catches" all the fish values. This collect mechanic is the core of the game and becomes significantly more powerful during free spins.

### Symbols and Payouts

**High Value Symbols**
- Fisherman (Wild) - highest paying regular symbol and collects Money Fish
- Tackle box
- Float
- Fishing rod

**Low Value Symbols**
- A, K, Q, J, 10 card royals

**Special Symbols**
- Fish Scatter - triggers free spins (3, 4, or 5 needed)
- Money Fish - carry random cash values for the fisherman to collect

## Free Spins Feature

### How to Trigger

Land 3, 4, or 5 Fish Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels:

- 3 Scatters = 10 free spins
- 4 Scatters = 15 free spins
- 5 Scatters = 20 free spins

On average, expect to trigger free spins roughly once every 160 to 200 spins, though this varies significantly due to the high volatility.

### Choose Your Modifier

When free spins trigger, you choose one of five modifiers. This is where Big Bass Splash separates itself from the rest of the series. Each modifier changes how the bonus round plays out:

| Modifier | What It Does |
|---|---|
| More Fish | Extra Money Fish symbols appear on the reels during free spins |
| More Fisherman | Extra Wild Fisherman symbols appear, increasing collect opportunities |
| Start from Level 2 | Skip Level 1 entirely and begin with a 2x multiplier on all wins |
| +2 Spins | Start with 2 extra free spins, plus 2 additional spins added to every retrigger |
| More Dynamites, Hooks and Bazookas | Increases the frequency of random bonus features during the round |

There is no objectively "best" modifier for every situation. However, **Start from Level 2** is widely considered the strongest option for chasing big wins because it immediately doubles all payouts and puts you closer to the 10x multiplier at Level 4.

## The Level-Up System

This is the mechanic that drives the biggest wins in Big Bass Splash. During free spins, collecting Fisherman Wilds advances you through four levels:

| Level | Multiplier | How to Reach |
|---|---|---|
| Level 1 | 1x | Starting level (unless you chose Level 2 modifier) |
| Level 2 | 2x | Collect 4 Fisherman Wilds |
| Level 3 | 3x | Collect 4 more Fisherman Wilds |
| Level 4 | 10x | Collect 4 more Fisherman Wilds |

Each time you advance a level, you also receive 10 additional free spins. Once you reach Level 4, retriggers stop, but the 10x multiplier applies to all remaining wins.

The jump from 3x at Level 3 to 10x at Level 4 is where the truly massive payouts happen. A decent Money Fish collection at Level 4 can deliver wins of several hundred times your stake in a single spin.

## Random Bonus Features

During free spins, three random features can appear at any time:

### Dynamite

An explosion drops new Money Fish symbols onto random positions across the grid. This can instantly fill the screen with collectible values, especially powerful when combined with a Fisherman Wild on the same spin.

### Hook

The hook reels in a missing symbol to complete a winning combination. If you are one Scatter short of a retrigger or one Fisherman short of a collect, the hook can save the day.

### Bazooka

The bazooka blasts the grid, replacing symbols with new ones. This can create entirely new winning combinations and Money Fish opportunities from what looked like a dead spin.

These random features keep free spins unpredictable and exciting even when the reels are not delivering on their own.

## RTP and Volatility

With an RTP of **96.71%**, Big Bass Splash sits comfortably above the industry average. The high volatility means:

- Most of your returns will come from the free spins feature, not the base game
- Expect long stretches of small or no wins between bonus triggers
- When the bonus does hit, particularly at higher levels, the payouts can be substantial
- Bankroll management is essential for sustained play

The 96.71% RTP is the default, but some operators may offer lower RTP versions. Always check the game information screen at your chosen casino to confirm the RTP before playing.

## Maximum Win Potential

The theoretical maximum win of **5,000x** your stake requires:

- Reaching Level 4 (10x multiplier)
- Multiple high-value Money Fish landing
- Fisherman Wilds collecting everything
- Extended free spins with retriggers

While 5,000x is the cap, wins in the 500x to 2,000x range are realistic outcomes from a strong Level 3 or Level 4 free spins round.

## Strategy Tips

### Bankroll Management

High volatility demands patience and discipline:

- Budget for at least 200 to 400 spins per session
- Set a loss limit before you start and stick to it
- Consider each bonus trigger as your opportunity; the base game is unlikely to deliver significant returns on its own

### Modifier Selection

Your modifier choice should match your strategy:

- **Start from Level 2** for maximum win potential (skipping the weakest level)
- **More Fisherman** for more collect opportunities throughout the round
- **+2 Spins** for longer sessions with more chances to reach higher levels
- **More Fish** for more values to collect when Fishermen do land
- **More Dynamites, Hooks and Bazookas** for unpredictable, explosive rounds

### Session Strategy

1. Set your stake at a level you are comfortable sustaining for a full session
2. Focus on reaching the free spins feature; the base game is low-return by design
3. When the modifier selection appears, choose based on your risk appetite
4. If you hit Level 3 or Level 4, stay calm and enjoy the multiplied wins
5. Bank significant wins rather than chasing even bigger ones
