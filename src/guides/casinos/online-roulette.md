## What Is Online Roulette?

Roulette is one of the oldest and most iconic casino games, and its online version faithfully recreates the experience. A ball spins around a numbered wheel, and you bet on where it will land. It's pure chance with no skill element, but understanding the different bet types and variants helps you make smarter choices.

## European vs American Roulette

This is the single most important decision you'll make when playing roulette online.

| Feature | European | American |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Numbers | 0-36 (37 pockets) | 0-36 plus 00 (38 pockets) |
| House Edge | 2.7% | 5.26% |
| Your Edge | Better | Worse |

The extra zero on the American wheel nearly doubles the house edge. There is no strategic reason to play American roulette when European is available. Always choose European.

### French Roulette: The Best Option

French roulette uses the European wheel but adds the La Partage rule: if you place an even-money bet and the ball lands on zero, you get half your stake back. This reduces the house edge on even-money bets to just 1.35%, making it the best roulette variant for players.

## Bet Types Explained

### Inside Bets (Higher Risk, Higher Reward)

- **Straight Up** - Bet on a single number. Pays 35:1
- **Split** - Bet on two adjacent numbers. Pays 17:1
- **Street** - Bet on a row of three numbers. Pays 11:1
- **Corner** - Bet on four numbers. Pays 8:1
- **Six Line** - Bet on two rows (six numbers). Pays 5:1

### Outside Bets (Lower Risk, Lower Reward)

- **Red/Black** - Bet on colour. Pays 1:1
- **Odd/Even** - Bet on odd or even numbers. Pays 1:1
- **High/Low** - Bet on 1-18 or 19-36. Pays 1:1
- **Dozens** - Bet on 1-12, 13-24, or 25-36. Pays 2:1
- **Columns** - Bet on a column of 12 numbers. Pays 2:1

Every bet type has the same house edge (2.7% on European roulette), so the choice is about your risk appetite, not mathematical advantage.

## Common Roulette Myths

### "The Martingale System Works"

The Martingale (doubling your bet after each loss) doesn't overcome the house edge. It increases the size of your losses when a losing streak hits, and table limits prevent unlimited doubling. No betting system changes the mathematical reality of roulette.

### "Numbers That Haven't Come Up Are Due"

Each spin is independent. The ball has no memory. A number that hasn't appeared in 100 spins is no more likely to appear on spin 101.

### "Dealers Can Control Where the Ball Lands"

In live roulette, dealer signature theory suggests patterns exist, but modern wheels are designed to be as random as possible. In RNG roulette, results are completely random.

## Tips for UK Players

1. **Play European or French roulette** exclusively; never American

3. **Set a session budget** and treat roulette as entertainment, not a money-making strategy
4. **Try live roulette** for the most engaging experience, but be aware of higher minimum bets
