## What Is Live Casino?

Live casino bridges the gap between online and land-based gambling. Real dealers operate real tables in professional studios, with every moment streamed to your device in HD. You place bets through your screen, but everything else is physical and happening in real time.

The result is an authentic casino experience from your sofa, complete with social interaction, atmospheric lighting, and the reassurance of watching every card dealt and every wheel spin.

## Live Casino Game Types

### Live Blackjack

The most strategic live casino game. Real dealers, real cards, and the same basic strategy applies as in RNG blackjack. Standard tables seat 7 players, while Infinite Blackjack allows unlimited players on the same hand.

**Key variants:**
- **Classic Blackjack** with standard 7-seat tables
- **Infinite Blackjack** for unlimited players (no waiting for a seat)
- **Speed Blackjack** with faster dealing and decision timers
- **Free Bet Blackjack** where the house pays for your doubles and splits

### Live Roulette

The most popular live casino game by volume. Watch a real ball spin on a real wheel with multiple camera angles.

**Key variants:**
- **Lightning Roulette** with random multipliers up to 500x
- **Immersive Roulette** with slow-motion replays
- **Speed Roulette** for faster results
- **Auto Roulette** with automated wheel (no dealer, faster pace)

### Live Baccarat

Punto Banco, the most common baccarat variant, is simple to play: bet on Player, Banker, or Tie. The Banker bet has the lowest house edge (1.06%) of any standard live casino bet.

**Key variants:**
- **Speed Baccarat** with 27-second game rounds
- **Baccarat Squeeze** where the dealer slowly reveals cards
- **Lightning Baccarat** with random multipliers

Evolution's game shows have revolutionised live casino, attracting players who'd never sit at a traditional table. These games are part entertainment, part gambling, with big personality dealers and flashy production values.
