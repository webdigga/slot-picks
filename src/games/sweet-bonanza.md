## How Sweet Bonanza Works

### Cluster Pays System

Sweet Bonanza doesn't use traditional paylines. Instead:

- Land **8 or more** matching symbols anywhere on the grid
- Symbols don't need to connect
- More matching symbols = higher payouts
- The 6x5 grid holds 30 symbol positions

### The Tumble Feature

After every win:
1. Winning symbols explode and disappear
2. Remaining symbols drop down
3. New symbols fall from above to fill gaps
4. Process repeats until no new wins form

This creates potential for multiple consecutive wins from a single bet.

## The Free Spins Feature

### How to Trigger

Land 4, 5, or 6 Lollipop Scatter symbols:
- 4 Scatters = 10 free spins
- 5 Scatters = 15 free spins
- 6 Scatters = 20 free spins

### Multiplier Bombs

During free spins, colourful bomb symbols can appear with multipliers ranging from **2x to 100x**:

- Bombs only appear during free spins
- Multiple bombs in one tumble sequence are **added together**
- The combined multiplier applies to the **total win** from that spin
- This is where the huge wins happen

### Retrigger Potential

Landing 3+ Scatters during free spins adds more spins:
- 3 Scatters = +5 free spins
- 4 Scatters = +10 free spins
- 5 Scatters = +15 free spins
- 6 Scatters = +20 free spins
