## Introduction to Starburst

Starburst is arguably the most famous online slot ever created. Released by NetEnt in 2012, this cosmic gem has remained a player favourite for over a decade. Its simplicity, combined with exciting expanding wilds and a stunning space theme, has made it a staple at virtually every online casino.

## How Starburst Works

### Basic Gameplay

Starburst features a classic 5-reel, 3-row layout with 10 fixed paylines. Unlike many modern slots, it pays both ways - meaning winning combinations can form from left to right AND right to left, effectively doubling your chances of landing a win.

### The Starburst Wild

The star of the show is the Starburst Wild symbol - a rainbow-coloured star that only appears on reels 2, 3, and 4. When it lands:

1. It expands to cover the entire reel
2. Triggers a free re-spin
3. Remains locked in place during the re-spin
4. If another wild lands, you get another re-spin (up to 3 re-spins total)

This feature is where the biggest wins come from, especially when multiple wilds expand simultaneously.
