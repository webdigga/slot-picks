## Introduction to Book of Dead

Book of Dead from Play'n GO has become one of the most popular slots in the UK market. Following the adventures of explorer Rich Wilde through ancient Egyptian tombs, this high-volatility slot offers the potential for massive wins up to 5,000x your stake.

## How Book of Dead Works

### Basic Gameplay

Book of Dead uses a classic 5-reel, 3-row layout with 10 adjustable paylines. The goal is to land matching symbols across active paylines from left to right, starting from reel 1.

### Symbols and Payouts

**High Value Symbols:**
- Rich Wilde (explorer) - highest paying
- Osiris
- Anubis
- Horus

**Low Value Symbols:**
- A, K, Q, J, 10 card royals

**Special Symbol:**
- Book of Dead - acts as both Wild and Scatter

## The Free Spins Feature

The main attraction in Book of Dead is the free spins bonus:

### How to Trigger
Land 3 or more Book of Dead symbols anywhere on the reels:
- 3 Books = 10 free spins
- 4 Books = 10 free spins + bigger win
- 5 Books = 10 free spins + massive win

### The Expanding Symbol
Before free spins begin, one symbol is randomly selected to become the "expanding symbol". When this symbol appears, it expands to cover the entire reel, creating opportunities for full-screen wins.

### Retrigger Potential
Landing 3+ Books during free spins adds another 10 spins to your total. This can happen repeatedly, up to a maximum of 100 free spins.

## RTP and Volatility Analysis

With an RTP of 96.21% and high volatility, Book of Dead is designed for players who can handle significant variance:

**What High Volatility Means:**
- Longer periods without wins
- When wins come, they can be substantial
- Requires a larger bankroll to weather dry spells
- Best suited for patient players

## Maximum Win Potential

The 5,000x maximum win can be achieved during free spins when:
- Rich Wilde is the expanding symbol
- Multiple reels fill with the expanding symbol
- The gamble feature is used to multiply wins

## Strategy Tips

### Bankroll Management
High volatility demands careful bankroll management:
- Set aside a dedicated session bankroll
- Use stakes that allow for at least 200-300 spins
- Accept that long losing streaks are normal

### Using the Gamble Feature
After any win, you can gamble to double (red/black) or quadruple (suit):
- Only gamble small wins you're comfortable losing
- Never gamble big wins from free spins
- Set a personal limit on consecutive gambles

### Payline Strategy
While you can adjust paylines from 1-10, most players keep all 10 active:
- More paylines = more chances to trigger the bonus
- Reducing paylines reduces overall RTP
