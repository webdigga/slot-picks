## That Prize Guy Review

That Prize Guy is a Northern Ireland-based online competition platform offering cash prizes, cars, holidays, and tech gadgets. With entries from 79p and over £143M awarded since 2020, it has become one of the UK's most popular prize competition sites.

### How It Works

Multiple competitions run simultaneously, each with a fixed number of entries. Once entries sell out or the draw date arrives, a winner is selected live on Facebook. You choose a competition, buy entries from 79p, answer a simple skill question (legal requirement), and watch the live draw.

### Prize Variety

The range is extensive: cash prizes from hundreds to tens of thousands, luxury cars, holidays, tech gadgets, and more. Most non-cash prizes offer a cash alternative, so you can take the money if you prefer.

### Transparency and Trust

All draws are live on Facebook, which is a significant transparency advantage. The platform holds a Trustpilot Excellent rating with reviews consistently praising fast payouts, responsive support, and genuine winners. Over £143M in prizes has been awarded since 2020.

### Odds and Value

Unlike lottery-style draws with unlimited entries, That Prize Guy caps entries per competition. This means your odds are known before entering. Competitions with fewer maximum entries naturally offer better individual chances.

### Our Verdict

That Prize Guy delivers on its promises with transparent live draws, diverse prizes, and a proven track record. The combination of low entry costs and capped entries makes it accessible while keeping odds reasonable. Set a budget and stick to it, as the low per-entry costs can add up.
