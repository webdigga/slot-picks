## Win A Million Review

Win A Million is a UK prize draw platform operated by Zebra Games Ltd, with the game format licensed by Sony Pictures Television. It offers cash prize draws five times per week with a top prize of £1,000,000 and entries starting from just 50p.

### How It Works

Each draw is live-streamed Wednesday to Sunday at 9pm. You buy entries from 50p per line, and results are determined by a random number generator certified by GLI (Gaming Laboratories International) and administered by Pims-SCA.

The headline "1 in 9 wins" figure refers to the odds of winning any prize across the tiered structure, not the jackpot specifically. Prize breakdowns are published for each individual draw before entry.

### Is It Legitimate?

Win A Million carries strong credibility markers. The Sony Pictures Television licence, GLI-certified RNG, and Pims-SCA administration are serious credentials. Free postal entry is available for every draw, confirming compliance with UK prize draw regulations.

### Value for Money

At 50p minimum entry, Win A Million is one of the most affordable prize draws in the UK. Compare this to National Lottery draws at £2 or Omaze entries from £10. The frequent draw schedule (five per week) also gives regular opportunities to play.

### Our Verdict

Win A Million combines low entry costs with credible licensing and transparent, live-streamed draws. The platform delivers genuine value for players who enjoy regular prize draw participation. Just keep your expectations realistic about jackpot odds, and set clear spending limits.
