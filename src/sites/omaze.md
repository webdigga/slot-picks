## Omaze UK Review

Omaze is a charity prize draw platform offering luxury houses, cash prizes, and more while raising money for UK charities. Since launching in the UK, Omaze has given away dozens of houses, made monthly millionaires, and raised over £93 million for charitable causes.

### How It Works

Browse active draws on omaze.co.uk, choose your entries (minimum £10 for house draws), and a portion of your entry fee goes directly to the named charity partner. Free postal entry is available for every draw. Winners are contacted directly after the draw date.

### The Prizes

House draws feature stunning UK properties worth £3M-£4M+, offered mortgage-free with stamp duty and legal fees covered. Winners typically also receive a cash lump sum of £50,000-£250,000. Monthly millionaire draws award £1,000,000 in cash.

### Charity Impact

Omaze partners with leading UK charities including Great Ormond Street Hospital, the Royal Marsden, Teenage Cancer Trust, RSPCA, and many more. Over £93 million has been raised through Omaze draws, funding research, patient care, and charitable programmes.

### Is It Legitimate?

Omaze operates as a prize draw with free postal entry, complying with UK regulations. House winners are well-documented with video reveals and press coverage. The platform publishes information about how entry fees are allocated between prizes, charity, and operations.

### Our Verdict

Omaze delivers a genuinely unique experience where your entry supports charity whether you win or not. The prizes are extraordinary and the charitable element gives real purpose to your spending. Entry costs are higher than other draws, but so is the potential reward.
