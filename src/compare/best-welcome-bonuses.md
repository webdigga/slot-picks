### True Value Calculation

**Expected Value Formula:**
Bonus Amount - (Total Wagering × House Edge) = Expected Value

**Example: £100 bonus, 35x wagering, 4% house edge**
- Total wagering: £3,500
- Expected loss: £3,500 × 4% = £140
- Expected value: £100 - £140 = -£40

This shows that high wagering can make bonuses mathematically negative value. The lower the wagering, the better.
