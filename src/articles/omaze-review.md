## What Is Omaze UK?

<a href="https://omaze.co.uk/?utm_source=slotpicks&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=omaze-review" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Omaze</a> is a charity prize draw platform that gives away luxury houses, cash prizes, and more while raising money for UK charities. Since launching in the UK, Omaze has given away dozens of mortgage-free homes, created monthly millionaires, and raised over £30M for charitable causes including Great Ormond Street Hospital, the Royal Marsden, and the RSPCA.

The concept is simple: you enter a prize draw, a portion of your entry fee goes to the named charity, and one winner takes home a life-changing prize. It's a model that originated in the US and has proven hugely popular in the UK since its launch.

## How Omaze Works

### Step 1: Choose a Draw

Browse active draws on the Omaze website. House draws are the flagship product, but monthly millionaire draws also run regularly. Each draw is tied to a specific charity partner.

### Step 2: Buy Entries

House draw entries start from £10, with bundle deals offering more entries at a lower per-entry cost. Monthly millionaire draws have different pricing.

### Step 3: Wait for the Draw

Once the draw date arrives, Omaze selects a winner. House draw winners are typically contacted directly and often feature in video reveals shared across Omaze's social channels and press coverage.

### Step 4: Free Postal Entry

Every Omaze draw offers free postal entry. You can enter without paying a penny. This is a legal requirement for UK prize draws, and Omaze provides clear instructions on their website for postal entries.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Omaze UK legit?

Yes. Omaze is a registered UK company that operates charity prize draws. Winners are publicly documented with video reveals and press coverage. Over £30M has been raised for UK charities including Great Ormond Street Hospital, the Royal Marsden, and the RSPCA. Free postal entry is available for every draw.

### How much does it cost to enter Omaze?

House draw entries start from £10 for 15 entries. Bundle deals reduce the per-entry cost: £25 for 50 entries (50p each) or £50 for 150 entries (33p each). Free postal entry is available for every draw.

### What happens if I win an Omaze house?

You receive the property mortgage-free with stamp duty and legal fees paid. A cash lump sum (typically £50,000 to £250,000) is included. You can choose to live in the house, rent it, or sell it.

### Does all my money go to charity with Omaze?

No. Your entry fee is split between the charity donation, prize costs, and operational expenses. The exact split varies by draw and is published on each draw page. If maximising charitable impact is your goal, direct donation is more efficient.

### What are my odds of winning an Omaze house?

Omaze does not publish entry numbers or odds for most draws. House draws are extremely popular, so individual odds of winning are very small. This is similar to any major prize draw or lottery.

### Can I enter Omaze for free?

Yes. Every Omaze draw offers free postal entry. Instructions are available on their website. Postal entries are included in the same draw as paid entries.

### How does Omaze choose its charity partners?

Omaze partners with established UK charities across healthcare, animal welfare, environmental causes, and more. Each draw names a specific charity partner, and funds raised are reported publicly.

### Is Omaze better than the National Lottery?

They serve different purposes. Omaze offers higher individual prize values (houses worth £4M+ vs average Lotto jackpots of ~£7.5M) but with unknown odds and higher entry costs. The National Lottery funds a wide range of good causes and is regulated by the UKGC. Both are legitimate ways to enter prize draws while supporting good causes.

### How is Omaze different from a lottery?

Omaze operates as a prize draw, not a lottery. The key legal distinction is that Omaze offers free postal entry, meaning no purchase is necessary to enter. Lotteries require payment to participate and are regulated by the UK Gambling Commission. Prize draws with a free entry route are not classified as gambling.

### Can I sell an Omaze house?

Yes. Once the property is legally transferred to you, it's yours to do with as you wish. You can live in it, rent it out, or sell it. Previous winners have chosen all three options.
